Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I specialize in web development, including frontend and backend development, UI/UX design, and responsive website creation. I also offer consulting services for technical projects.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 2-3 months or more. I'll provide a detailed timeline after discussing your requirements.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes! I work with clients from all over the world. I'm experienced in remote collaboration and use tools like Slack, Zoom, and project management software to ensure smooth communication.

What is your development process?

I follow an agile approach: discovery and planning, design and prototyping, development and testing, deployment, and ongoing support. You'll be involved throughout the process with regular updates.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, I offer maintenance and support packages for all projects. This includes bug fixes, updates, and minor modifications to ensure your website continues to run smoothly.

What are your payment terms?

I typically work with a 50% upfront payment to begin the project and 50% upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. Payment methods include bank transfer and PayPal.
